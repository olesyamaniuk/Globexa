FAQ

  • How are projects delivered?
    All consulting and systems work is delivered online via Loom, Notion, and shared tools. We structure work into short sprints — typically 3, 7, or 10 days — with async check-ins. At the end, you receive a full system walkthrough and implementation manual
  • What tools do you specialize in?
    We work with Notion, ClickUp, Airtable, Tally, Zapier, Make, Google Workspace, and other modern stack tools. We adapt to your existing setup and suggest lean upgrades when needed — no unnecessary complexity
  • Is this for new or experienced founders?
    Both. If you're launching your first digital product, we’ll help you set it up right from the start. If you’ve already launched but are overwhelmed, we’ll restructure your backend so you can scale efficiently and calmly
  • Do I need to be available for live meetings?
    No. Our process is designed to be async-friendly. You’ll get recorded walkthroughs, comment threads, and occasional optional calls. This means you can focus on your business — not scheduling meetings
  • Can you build and automate funnels for my offer?
    Yes. We create smart, minimal funnels using tools like ConvertKit, Tally, and Zapier. Whether it’s a course, newsletter, or services funnel — we design the flow, set up the tools, and hand you a tested system
  • Do you provide ongoing support after the sprint?
    We offer optional retainers for monthly support, updates, and optimizations. Some clients work with us once, others stay for months as their fractional systems partner. You're in control
