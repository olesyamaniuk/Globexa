FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does it take to implement a full system?
    A typical build takes between 3 to 10 days, depending on complexity. We work in intensive sprints with a clear timeline, so you’re never guessing when things will be delivered
  • Do I need to be tech-savvy to work with you?
    Not at all. We use beginner-friendly no-code tools and provide training walkthroughs for each setup. Our aim is to make you independent, not overwhelmed
  • What tools do you work with?
    We specialize in Notion, ClickUp, Zapier, Tally, Airtable, and more. If you already have tools you prefer, we’ll adapt — otherwise, we’ll guide you toward the best fit
  • Can you help with localization for international markets?
    Yes. We can help you create simple localization frameworks, adapt content tone, and suggest UX improvements that work across cultures
  • What if I need ongoing support?
    We offer monthly retainers and async coaching plans if you need continued guidance. Or you can book us for follow-up sprints anytime
  • Do you provide templates I can use myself?
    Yes! We sell Notion, Airtable, and funnel templates that are plug-and-play — ideal if you want to build on your own but with expert scaffolding
